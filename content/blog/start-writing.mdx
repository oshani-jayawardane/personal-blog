## A Small Reflection

It was a small pause between my thoughts. A late night. I had just finished watching a beautifully unsettling film. 
Another post for another day. Suddenly, I had become a movie critic. I wanted to discuss it. 
I felt the urge to share with someone what I saw, my view, my opinion. Why? 
Because you don't just see with your eyes and hear with your ears. 
You feel and experience a work of art with all your senses. You absorb it. You carry it. 
Suddenly, it occurred to me all at once: “I should start a blog!”

I have been thinking about building a professional portfolio for a while. Something polished. Something presentable. 
But what is a professional life without a bit of creativity and a pocket of curiosity? Yes! That is what I want my blog to exactly be. 
A human. A refelction. An imperfection. A gentle confession. A humble revelation. A perfect balance.

---

## What's Next

I will write! I will write about what moves me. A movie that keeps me up at night. I will write about what keeps me busy. 
A paper that shifts my thinking. A project that stretches me too thin. A question that lingers longer than it should. This space will grow as I grow. 
I will keep noticing the most amazing things in the world, and I will keep writing. See you soon.
